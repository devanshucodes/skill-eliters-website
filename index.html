Skill Eliters
Shaping budding Entrepreneurs

We at Skill Eliters are on a mission to help the budding entrepreneurs in converting their idea into a full fledged startup by providing them technical support . We are a community of 30+ members currently, all our members are currently being trained to make industry ready projects. Our main objective is to lit the spirit of entrepreneurship among the youths and use their revolutionary ideas to solve the real world problems. Henceforth we invite all of you to come and join us in revolutionising the world.


------------- Who are we ? ------------

Mission

We at Skill Eliters are on a mission to create entrepreneurs who can solve the real world problems by their revolutionary ideas. We have got immense of talent in our country the only problem lies during the initial phases of their journey, due to lack of awareness and mentorship people generally give up very easily. We at Skill Eliters are going to support the budding entrepreneurs by providing them the necessary guidance , mentoring and technical support.

Value

Our idea of supporting the budding entrepreneurs is going to bring a great shift in the mindset of the common people. Our dream is to change the mindset of seeking jobs to providing jobs, so that there is less pressure on the system and our country as a whole can grow up all together, also the entrepreneurial mindset is going to improve the quality of products and services of our country upto a great extent.

Vision

Our vision is to bring all the like minded people having entrepreneurial mindset together , providing them projects to work on so that even if they are starting from scratch, still can get ready to serve the industry, these people are eventually going to motivate others and hence will lead to expansion of our community as well as the entrepreneurial mindset, all these industry ready entrepreneurs will help the people coming up with various startup ideas in providing them technical support and mentorship, so that the spirit of entrepreneurship wins at the end.
